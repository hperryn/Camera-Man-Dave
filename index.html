Meet Dave

Dave is the dedicated videographer for Astley and Buckshaw United, capturing every thrilling moment of their matches each Saturday. A long-standing member of the club, he also sits on the committee and previously served as treasurer, demonstrating his deep commitment both on and off the pitch. Dave’s talents extend beyond matchday filming—his work is regularly featured on social media, where he manages content for both his personal accounts and the club’s X profile, sharing highlights, polls, and updates with the community. His skills have been recognized beyond the club, most recently being invited to film a prestigious cup final. Passionate about supporting the team and giving back, Dave frequently organizes fundraisers for new equipment and for causes close to the club, including Genesis Care NW.
